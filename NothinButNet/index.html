Core Features


* Realistic basketball handling mechanics such as dribbling, spinning / balancing the ball and shooting from various angles and strength.
* Enjoy shooting hoops on your own in the single player practice mode.
* Challenge your friends in a game of "horse" with a word of your choice.
* Prove your skills in a timed competition mode in both single player and multiplayer.
* Jump into a casual game of shooting hoops with your friends in multiplayer casual mode.
* Set the record score on how long you can spin and balance the ball your controller.
