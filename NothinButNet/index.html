Core Features


* Realistic basketball handling mechanics such as dribbling, spinning / balancing, and shooting from various angles and strength.
* Enjoy shooting hoops on your own in solo Free Throw or Rapid Fire.
* Challenge your friends in a game of H.O.R.S.E with a word of your choice, compete for the highest score in Rapid Fire or see who can score a basket the quickest in Knockout.
* Jump into a casual game of shooting hoops with your friends in online Free Throw.
* Prove your skills by setting a record on how long you can spin and balance the ball on your controller. (Sounds a lot easier than it is!)
