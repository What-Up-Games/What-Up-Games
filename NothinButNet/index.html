Each round, you or a friend will need to invent a new shot. If made, everyone else will need to take the exact same shot. Anyone who misses their shot will get a letter and will become eliminated once the word has been completed. The last player standing wins! H.O.R.S.E. is only available online and is played with 2-4 players. - title: Knockout description: Complete against your friends to see who can make a shot the quickest or risk being eliminated! In Knockout, each player will line up at the free throw line. The first player in line will have one chance to make a basket. If they miss, the second player in line will begin shooting. The goal is to score a basket before the other player does! If the first player in line scores, they are safe and move to the back of the line. However, if the second player in line scores, the first player is eliminated. Each round begins the same, with new players in the first and second positions. The last player standing wins! Knockout is only available online and is played with 2-4 players. - title: Shot Blocker description: Put your reflexes to the test in Shot Blocker! Take turns blocking or shooting the ball. Points are gained by successfully scoring a basket. Points can be denied by successfully blocking the shot. The first player to bank three points wins. Shot Blocker is only available online and is played with 2 players. - title: Rapid Fire description: Compete to score the highest amount of points within the time limit. Utilize the entire court to make two or three point shots. In Rapid Fire, you can challenge up to three of your friends to see who is better in real time. Or, if you prefer to play solo you can compete to see how you rank on the leaderboard. Rapid Fire can be played with 1-4 players. - title: Free Throw description: Practice makes perfect, especially when playing Nothin’ But Net! Free Throw mode is without rules, letting you practice dribbling, shooting and balancing either online with friends or by yourself. Free Throw can be played with 1-4 players. ---
